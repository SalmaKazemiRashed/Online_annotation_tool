Level 3: Spot or not (intermidiate)

Cells can die in many ways and understanding the type of cell death is important to study the function of genes and drugs so we can make the best targeted treatments. Our cells have been stained with two different dyes that allow us to distinguish the type of cell death. One of the dyes changes appearance from even to spotty when the cell is dying because the dye recognizes the damage of specific cell structures called lysosomes (read more here). So, in level 3, you simply need to tell us if there are spots in the cell or not. See here for examples on how to classify in level 3 and start Practicing or or start the game directly
