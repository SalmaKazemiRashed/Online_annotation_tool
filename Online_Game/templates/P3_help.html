Level 3

Cells can die in many ways and understanding the type of cell death is important to study the function of genes and drugs so we can make the best targeted treatments. Our cells have been stained with two different dyes that allow us to distinguish the type of cell death. This specific cell death dye changes appearance from even to spotty when the cell is dying because it recognizes the damage of specific cell structures called lysosomes and accumulates there.
