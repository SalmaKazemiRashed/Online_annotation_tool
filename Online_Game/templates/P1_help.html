Level 1

In this level, you help us detect images of poor quality, e.g. which are out of focus or which contain dust particles which sometimes sneak into the microscope. These images need to be sorted out because they cannot give us meaningful information about the cells. Here are a few examples and how to classify them. It is important to consider that the "Empty" or "no objects" labels should address those images that contain no objects in the center of the image (under the red dot in the main page of the game).
