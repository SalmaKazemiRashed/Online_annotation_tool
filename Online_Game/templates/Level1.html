Level 1: Good image or bad image

Here you help us detect images of poor quality, e.g. which are out of focus or which contain dust particles which sometimes sneak into the microscope. These images need to be sorted out because they cannot give us meaningful information about the cells. Spotting these flaws is easy for a human but we need thousands of images to teach our artificial intelligence system because computers are less clever. So, give it a try! See here for examples on how to classify in level 1 and start Practicing or start the game directly.
