Level 2

The nucleus is the part of the cell in which the genetic information (DNA) is stored and in our images we marked it with a specific dye. In cells that are stressed or dying the nucleus often changes its appearance. You are tasked with deciding if a nucleus looks normal or strange so we can find out if a treatment harms a cell. Here are a few examples and how to classify them. It is important to consider that the "no object" label should address those images that contain no objects in the center of the image (under the red dot in the main page of the game).
