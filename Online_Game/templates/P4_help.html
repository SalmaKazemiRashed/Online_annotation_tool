Level 4

As you probably know by now, cells can die in many ways and understanding the type of cell death is important to study the function of genes and drugs so we can make the best targeted treatments. Here the cells have been stained with a cell death dye that normally is confined to specific cell compartments called mitochondria (read more here) but leaks out when they have been damaged. So, you need to decide if some of the dye has leaked and is diffusely spread out in the cell. It is important to consider that the "no object" label should address those images that contain no objects in the center of the image (under the red dot in the main page of the game).
