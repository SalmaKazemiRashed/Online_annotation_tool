Who are we?

The Cell Death and Lysosomes Group is part of Lund University in Sweden. As the name suggests we are interested in all things related to cell death. Our main research goal is understanding this fundamental process and its role in different diseases and developing therapies that can fix cell death when it is broken. Besides this, our group is passionate about bringing science out to the general public and in supporting the vibrant artificial intelligence community in Lund.

You can also check out our lab website (www.aitslab.org) and university research profile.
