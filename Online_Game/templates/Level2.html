Level 2: Normal or strange nucleus (easy)

The nucleus is the part of the cell in which the genetic information is stored and in our images we marked it with a specific dye. In cells that are stressed or dying the nucleus often changes its appearance and by deciding if a nucleus looks normal or funny we can find out if a treatment harms a cell. It will be easy for you to spot the difference. See here for examples on how to classify in level 2 and start Practicing or start the game directly
